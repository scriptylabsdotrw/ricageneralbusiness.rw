About us

Lanova is a translation and communication company established in 2023. Our ultimate goal is to help our clients communicate effectively across borders and cultures, enabling them to succeed in today's global marketplace. Lanova provides businesses and individuals with professional translation and communication services. We empower our clients to achieve greater global success in more than 10 languages including Kinyarwanda, English, French, Kiswahili, and more.
